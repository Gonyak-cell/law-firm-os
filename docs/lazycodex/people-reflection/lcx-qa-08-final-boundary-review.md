# LCX-QA-08 Final Boundary Review

Date: 2026-06-24
Program: `LCX-PPL Full Reflection`

## Verdict

`local_runtime_ready_candidate_only`

The LCX-PPL People expansion may claim local runtime-ready candidate status
after the final validator and browser QA receipt pass. It may not claim
production readiness, go-live approval, enterprise trust, external provider
readiness, legal owner approval, or AI final decision authority.

## Allowed Claim

`LCX-PPL local runtime-ready candidate complete; production, go-live, and enterprise trust claims remain false.`

## Disallowed Claims

| Claim | State |
| --- | --- |
| Production ready | false |
| Go-live approved | false |
| Enterprise trust approved | false |
| External provider ready | false |
| Legal owner approved | false |
| AI final decision allowed | false |

## Upgrade Evidence Required

- Production deployment receipt and environment-specific smoke evidence.
- Legal owner approval for conflict, ethical-wall, and sensitive People access
  policies.
- External provider readiness receipts where provider-backed HR or legal
  integrations are claimed.
- Enterprise trust/security review receipts before enterprise trust claims.
- Go-live approval separate from local runtime QA.
