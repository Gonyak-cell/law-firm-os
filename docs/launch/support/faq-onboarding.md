## Boundary

This document is a support and onboarding draft only. It does not open a
support channel, does not publish user-facing training, does not send launch
communications, does not perform a permission-request demonstration, does not
create audit events, and does not claim G6 support-channel evidence.

Final publication depends on LT-L4-W06 training source materials, owner-approved
support channel routing, and a live permission request demonstration under
LT-L6-W02-T02.

## Support Channel Intake Template

| Field | Required value |
| --- | --- |

| Routing note | support, incident, permission request, break-glass, change, training, or backlog |

## FAQ Draft

| FAQ ID | Question | Draft answer | Route |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| FAQ-01 | I cannot access a matter I expect to see. | Use the permission request process; do not share matter data outside approved channels. | permission_request |
| FAQ-02 | I see a permission denied screen during urgent work. | Capture the safe error reference and route to support; emergency access uses break-glass only after approval. | support_or_break_glass |
| FAQ-03 | An email filing recommendation looks wrong. | Do not file blindly; verify the selected matter and issue/task/deadline outputs before confirming. | training_or_support |
| FAQ-04 | A filed email or document appears on the wrong matter. | Stop further filing, open a support request, preserve audit references, and do not delete evidence manually. | support_incident |
| FAQ-05 | The Work Queue item is assigned to the wrong person. | Use the approved workflow correction path once runtime exists; do not edit database or local fixtures. | support_permission_or_workflow |
| FAQ-06 | A document version or status is unclear. | Check Document Workspace metadata and route unclear status/QC issues to support. | support_training |
| FAQ-07 | Search results are missing a document. | Confirm matter access first; search must remain permission-trimmed and cannot be bypassed. | support_or_permission |
| FAQ-08 | Outlook Add-in pane is not loading. | Capture mailbox/test environment, pane error, and timestamp; route to support without forwarding client content. | support_incident |
| FAQ-09 | AI features are unavailable. | Wave 1 keeps AI dark/off; AI activation waits for release-gate and reviewer approvals. | training |
| FAQ-10 | I need a new role or temporary access. | Submit a permission request with reason, duration, target scope, and approval basis. | permission_request |
| FAQ-11 | I suspect a security or confidentiality issue. | Treat as incident; preserve evidence and avoid spreading sensitive content. | incident |
| FAQ-12 | I need help during pilot training. | Use the approved support channel after it is opened; questions should be logged for FAQ improvement. | support_training |
