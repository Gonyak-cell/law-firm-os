## M&A Priority Rationale

M&A is the recommended first pilot lane because OQ-015 explicitly asks whether
MVP practice packs should start with M&A, and the launch plan calls for M&A
priority review. The M&A pack also exercises the widest Wave 1 workflow mix:
matter overview, document/RFI tracking, DD issue capture, closing checklist,
third-party consent tracking, Outlook filing, task/deadline coordination, and
closing bible assembly.
