# Law Firm OS Roadmap v0.1

## Phase 0: Product Constitution And Architecture

Goal: freeze shared identity, security, data ownership, and MVP scope before feature coding.

Outputs:

- Product vision.
- Domain model.
- Taxonomy v1.
- Security model.
- Finance policy.
- AI governance policy.
- Compliance baseline.
- MVP scope.
- Data migration policy.
- Integration strategy.

## Phase 1: Matter Core And DMS Core MVP

P0 scope:

- Tenant, User, Role.
- Client and Matter.
- Matter Team.
- Matter Workspace.
- Document upload and download.
- Version management.
- Basic search.
- Matter-team permission.
- Audit log.
- Matter dashboard.

## Phase 2: Time, Billing, Expense MVP

P0 scope:

- Engagement terms.
- Time entry.
- Expense and disbursement.
- Proforma.
- Invoice.
- Payment.
- AR aging.
- DMS link.
- Basic Matter P&L.
- Monthly closing.

## Phase 3: CRM, Intake, Conflict

Scope:

- Contact.
- Opportunity.
- Proposal.
- Activity.
- Referral and origination credit.
- Conflict check.
- Engagement approval.
- Opportunity to Matter conversion.
- Pipeline dashboard.

## Phase 4: Email And Office Native DMS

Scope:

- Outlook filing.
- Gmail filing.
- Email thread management.
- Attachment extraction.
- Office add-in.
- Check-in/out.
- Document compare.
- Secure external sharing.

## Phase 5: Settlement, Analytics, Governance

Scope:

- Settlement rules.
- Origination credit.
- Partner allocation.
- Monthly settlement.
- Advanced P&L.
- DLP.
- Retention and legal hold.
- SSO/MFA.
- Audit analytics.

## Phase 6: AI Legal Knowledge Platform

Scope:

- Matter AI search.
- Precedent search.
- Clause library.
- Markup analyzer.
- DD issue extraction.
- Litigation assist.
- Report generator.
- Billing narrative AI.
- Partner analytics AI.
