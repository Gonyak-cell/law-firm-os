# Closeout Packs

Law Firm OS closeout now operates through risk-sized Closeout Packs instead of one-by-one subphase commits as the primary execution unit.

The expanded product plan tracks 55,256 implementation units: 54,355 existing Law Firm OS units plus 901 embedded HRX / People Evidence units. HRX remains inside Law Firm OS as an embedded People / HR Evidence module, not as a separate product.

Closeout Packs are not equal arithmetic buckets. The approximate 1,000-pack operating estimate is risk based and may land in the 760-1,295 range. Risk A packs cover 1-10 units, Risk B packs cover 10-40 units, and Risk C packs cover 40-150 units.

Every production-ready pack must include implementation, tests, Hermes evidence, one Claude Opus 4.8 max read-only pack review, finding adjudication, construction inspection, included units marked production_ready, and a commit.
